# Digital Marketing Strategy: Shri Adinath Farm (Bhopal)

**Objective:** Establish a strong digital presence, generate high-quality leads, and drive sales for farmhouse plots in Bhopal through organic content (Reels) and paid advertising.

---

## 1. Brand Positioning
*   **Tagline Ideas:** 
    *   "Nature's Luxury, Just a Drive Away."
    *   "Own Your Peace in Bhopal."
    *   "Shri Adinath Farm: Where Investment Meets Serenity."
*   **Key Selling Points (USPs):**
    *   Proximity to Bhopal city (Accessibility).
    *   Greenery/Nature focus.
    *   Appreciation value (Investment).
    *   Amenities (Security, Water, Electricity, Gated Community).

## 3. Lead Generation (Paid Ads)
**Platform:** Facebook & Instagram Lead Forms (Meta Ads).

### **Target Audience:**
*   **Location:** Bhopal + 50km radius (exclude very rural areas if targeting premium buyers).
*   **Age:** 28 - 55 (Key earning demographic).
*   **Interests:** 
    *   Real Estate Investing
    *   Luxury lifestyle
    *   Nature lovers / Gardening
    *   Competitor pages (e.g., other renowned Bhopal townships).

### **Ad Creatives:**
*   **Carousel Ad:** 5 Images (Entrance Gate, Plantation, Plot View, Map Location, Layout Plan).
*   **Video Ad:** 15-second teaser of the Reel #1.

### **Lead Form Fields:**
*   Name
*   Phone Number (Pre-filled)
*   "Are you looking for Investment or Personal Use?" (Qualifying Question).

---

## 4. Sales Funnel
1.  **Awareness:** User sees Reel/Ad.
2.  **Interest:** User clicks "Learn More" or messages on WhatsApp.
3.  **Consideration:** You send the **Digital Brochure** (PDF) + Walkthrough Video.
4.  **Action:** User visits the site (Site Visit).
5.  **Conversion:** Booking.
